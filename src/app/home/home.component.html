 WELCOME TO KALAKRUTHI

Kalakruthi Art Galleria began its journey in the first decade of new millennium and geared up an evoution in the mordern and contemporary art in India. Kalakruthi has showcased some of the unique and finest exhibitions of mordern and contemporary art in India building up a gateway of Indian art in the world market. Kalakruthi Art Gallery presently operates from two places -one gallery is in Kolkata and the other is in New Delhi
